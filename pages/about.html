ABOUT US
Our recipes are based on the great english chef, Yottam Ottolenghi, who has developed a style of food which is rooted in Middle Eastern and Mediterranean traditions, but which also draws in diverse influences and ingredients from around the world.

Prosto ('simple' in Polish) comes from one of his bestselling books.
ORGNIC FOOD
We pride ourselves on using mainly and when possible organic food. Ingredients produced without using antibiotics in animal feed, genetically engineered organisms, chemical preservatives, radiation, or artificial pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers.
LOCAL PROVIDERS
On the same line, we've also developed a great network of local providers, vendors and contacts. This helps lifting local economy as well as reducing the carbon footprint.
